Yoga & Wellness Studio

Site Planning Document

Site Name

Yoga & Wellness Studio – This name reflects the brand’s commitment to promoting holistic health through yoga, mindfulness, and wellness practices. It's memorable, professional, and aligned with our studio's mission.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an informational and functional hub for current and prospective members. It provides details about our wellness philosophy, class schedules, instructor biographies, and allows users to book sessions or reach out with questions.

Scenarios

  • What yoga classes are available on weekday evenings?
  • How can I contact a specific instructor or learn about their background?

Color Schema

  • Serenity Blue (#88c9bf) – Used for headers and navigation backgrounds for a calming feel.
  • Earthy Beige (#f5f1e7) – Used for the page background and content blocks to create a grounded, natural look.

Typography

  • 'Playfair Display' – Used for main headers to give a sophisticated, wellness-centric tone.
  • 'Open Sans' – Used for body text for readability and modern look.
